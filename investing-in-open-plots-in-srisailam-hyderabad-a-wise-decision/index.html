1. Srisailam Highway – A Brief Overview

Srisailam Highway, also known as NH 765, is a 270 km national highway that runs through the states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. It starts in Hyderabad and ends in Srisailam, a holy town that attracts thousands of tourists and pilgrims every year. Along the way, it passes through several towns and villages, making it an important lifeline for the people living in these areas.

Srisailam Highway is also an important commercial corridor that connects Hyderabad to the rest of the country. It is well-connected to other major highways, such as NH 44 and NH 65, and is close to the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, making it a convenient location for businesses and industries.

2. Futuristic Potential of Srisailam Highway

The Srisailam Highway has the potential to become an important transportation hub in the future due to its strategic location and increased development in the areas around it. The expansion of areas nearby like Lemoor, Rachloor, and Tukuguda will increase the demand for efficient transportation infrastructure, making the Srisailam highway a critical route for both commuters and freight transit, as well as an attractive investment location for future growth.

The need for housing and business space is likely to rise as the region surrounding the Srisailam Highway grows and develops. Real estate developers have already begun to invest in projects along this roadway, which include residential flats, villas, and commercial areas such as malls and office buildings.

The Srisailam Highway is also close to various industrial zones, information technology parks, and educational institutions. This makes it appealing to those who work in these industries and wish to reside in an easily accessible area.

In the future, we could see smart traffic management systems implementing advanced technologies such as autonomous vehicles to enhance the efficiency and safety of the highway. The development of new businesses and industries along the route could also lead to economic growth and job opportunities for the local population.

Overall, the future of real estate on the Srisailam Highway appears promising, and it is set to become a popular area for both residential and commercial development in the coming years.

4. Future Prospects

The Srisailam Highway area in Hyderabad is rapidly becoming a hub for industrial and electronic manufacturing projects. Several major developments, including E-City (Fab City), SCL Electronics, and WIPRO’s 20-acre Electronic System Design and Manufacturing (ESDM) clusters are located in the area. The ESDM Park offers facilities for electronics manufacturing and design firms, including labs, an incubation center for startups, and a common facility center. 

In addition, there are several other electronic and hardware manufacturing companies in the area which are all located within a 15-minute drive of each other. The area is also close to other significant developments like Hyderabad Pharma City and Eliminedu Tata Aero Space & Defense Park.

The Telangana state government has been implementing several initiatives to promote investment in the state. The Srisailam Highway Aerospace SEZ

The Srisailam Highway Aerospace SEZ, which was developed by the Telangana State Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (TSIIC), is currently home to numerous aerospace companies.

These include Tata Boeing Aerospace Ltd, Adani Aerospace Ltd, Asaco Pvt Ltd, Nucon Aerospace Limited, Gowra Aerospace Technologies, Tata Advanced Systems, Tata Sikorsky Aerospace Ltd, and Sri Venkateshwara Aerospace Ltd, among others.

The Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

This Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is located close to Srisailam Highway and is expected further to boost the real estate market in the area. Furthermore, the airport’s position in Hyderabad, a fast-rising metropolitan city with a booming IT industry, might boost its ability to attract investments and enterprises, resulting in increased land prices in surrounding areas on the srisailam highway in the long term.

The Hyderabad Pharma City

Hyderabad Pharma City is a project initiated by the Government of Telangana to focus on the life sciences and pharmaceutical sector. The government’s new industrial policy aims to develop this sector and provide a conducive environment for its growth. The project has been granted NIMZ (National Investment and Manufacturing Zone) status by the Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP) in recognition of the government’s vision and efforts.

The Department of Pharmaceuticals is also keen to support the project, which requires 19,333 acres of land and an estimated budget of Rs. 16,395 crores. The Pharma City has an estimated investment potential of Rs. 64,000 crores and an expected export potential of Rs. 58,000 crores per annum. It is expected to provide direct employment to 150,000 people and indirect employment to 500,000.

Hyderabad Pharma City aims to create an integrated pharma manufacturing ecosystem that includes NIMZ Manufacturing Units, a Pharma University, a Research & Development Centre, and a Township for living. The majority of the land will be used for pharmaceutical manufacturing, while the rest will be developed for a pharmaceutical university, an R&D and ancillary hub, and a pharmaceutical city township with all necessary social and physical infrastructure.
